## ğŸ“œ GAME RULES (Yes, there are rules)

RoleMIAster uses a classic and robust d100 (hundred-sided die) system.

### 1. Your Attributes (What defines you)
They are generated randomly, just like in classic tabletop RPGs!

*   ğŸ’ª **Strength (STR):** For hitting hard and lifting stones.
*   â¤ï¸ **Constitution (CON):** For enduring hits, diseases, and poisons.
*   ğŸ˜ **Size (SIZ):** Big and heavy, or small and nimble.
*   ğŸƒ **Dexterity (DEX):** Agility, aiming, and not tripping over your own feet.
*   ğŸ§  **Intelligence (INT):** Learning, remembering, and solving puzzles.
*   ğŸ”® **Power (POW):** Your soul, your luck, willpower, and magic.
*   ğŸ˜ **Charisma (CHA):** Leadership, attractiveness, and ability to trick people.

### 2. Quick Math (Derived Characteristics)
*   **Health (HP):** `(CON + SIZ) / 2`. If it reaches 0... well, you know. F.
*   **Mana (MP):** Equal to your POW. No mana, no tricks. (A wizard without mana is just a guy in a robe cosplay).
*   **Action Points (AP):** `(INT + DEX) / 2`. Determines your Initiative. The one who strikes first, strikes twice.

### 3. Your Skills
They start with a base value (e.g., Stealth = DEX + INT) and improve with use. It's a percentage (%).

### 4. Action Resolution (The Dice)
When you try to do something risky (climbing, lying, attacking...), the AI will ask for a roll.
The game rolls an invisible **d100**. **(Remember: The dice hate you. It's personal).**

*   **Your Goal:** Roll **LESS THAN OR EQUAL TO** your skill.
    *   *Example:* You have 45% in Climb.
    *   You roll a 30: âœ… **Success**. You climb like a monkey.
    *   You roll an 80: âŒ **Failure**. You take a nasty fall.
    *   You roll a 01-05: âœ¨ **CRITICAL!** You perform epic parkour with a flip.
